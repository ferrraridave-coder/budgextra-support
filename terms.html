1. Description of the App

Budgextra is a personal budgeting app for iPhone and iPad that helps you track monthly bills, expenses, income, and balances.

All data is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synced across your Apple devices using Apple’s iCloud (CloudKit).

Key features include:

  • Monthly bill tracking with due dates and payment status
  • Manual expense tracking
  • Income recording
  • Balance calculation with automatic carry-over between months
  • Optional overdraft tracking
  • Manual balance adjustments
  • Optional Face ID protection
  • Optional iCloud sync and backup

The App works fully offline. An internet connection is only required for iCloud sync when enabled.

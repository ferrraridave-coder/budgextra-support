1. Description of the Service

Budgextra is a personal budgeting app for iPhone, iPad & Mac that allows you to track monthly bills, expenses, and income. The App stores all data locally on your device and optionally syncs across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit).

Key features include:

  • Monthly bill tracking with due dates and payment status
  • Manual expense tracking
  • Income recording
  • Balance calculation with carry-over between months
  • Optional overdraft tracking
  • Manual balance adjustments
  • Optional Face ID protection
  • Optional iCloud sync across devices

The App does not require an internet connection to function. All core features work fully offline. iCloud sync occurs automatically when a connection is available.

Budgextra — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Budgextra ("the App") is developed by David Picknell. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use Budgextra on iPhone, iPad & Mac.

Data Collection

Budgextra does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your financial data — including bills, income, expenses, balance adjustments, and settings — is stored locally on your device.

iCloud (CloudKit) Sync

If you are signed in to iCloud on your device, Budgextra uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your Apple devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. Apple's iCloud is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Local Storage

All data is saved locally on your device using JSON files stored in the app's sandboxed container. This data persists between app launches and is not shared with any external service.

Face ID

Budgextra offers an optional Face ID lock to protect access to your financial data. Face ID authentication is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework on your device. No biometric data is accessed, stored, or transmitted by the App.

Analytics & Tracking

Budgextra does not use any analytics, tracking, advertising frameworks, or third-party SDKs. No usage data, crash reports, or behavioural data is collected by the developer.

Third-Party Services

Budgextra does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or advertising networks. The only external service used is Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) for optional cross-device sync.

Children's Privacy

Budgextra does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App does not require any account creation or personal data entry beyond your own financial records stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
